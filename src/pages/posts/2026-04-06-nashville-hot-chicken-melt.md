# Ingredients

- 2 lbs chicken breast, thinly sliced
- 1 tbsp oil (plus 1 tbsp more for cooking)
- 1/4 cup Nashville Hot seasoning (divided)
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp pickle brine
- 2 hoagie rolls
- 1 tbsp chopped chipotles (optional)
- 4 slices pepper jack cheese
- Pickles (for garnish)

# Instructions

1. **Prep the Chicken:** In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced chicken breast with 1 tablespoon of oil and 2 tablespoons of the Nashville Hot seasoning. Mix well to ensure the chicken is coated.
2. **Mix the Spicy Mayo:** In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, pickle brine, and the remaining Nashville Hot seasoning. Taste and adjust.
3. **Toast Rolls:** Heat a griddle or pan to medium heat. Place the hoagie rolls face down and toast them for a few minutes until slightly crispy. 
4. **Sear the Chicken:** Add another tablespoon of oil to the hot griddle. Spread the seasoned chicken out and sear on one side for a few minutes before flipping. Continue flipping as needed until the chicken is almost fully cooked (around 160°F).
5. **Add Chipotle & Cheese:** Before the chicken is completely cooked, add the chopped chipotles (if using) and lay the pepper jack cheese slices over the chicken. Mix and chop everything with your spatula to finish cooking the chicken and melt the cheese.
6. **Assemble and Serve:** Assemble the sandwiches by placing the seared, cheesy chicken onto the toasted hoagie rolls. Garnish with pickles and top with the spicy mayo!
