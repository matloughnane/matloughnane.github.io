This is a recipe found on instagram by [@thegoodbite](https://www.instagram.com/reels/DTOLG5VjK8g/)
(Makes 2 servings. 54g protein, 688 calories per serving)

# Ingredients

- 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 1 tbsp cornflour (cornstarch)
- 1 tbsp oil from a jar of sun-dried tomatoes
- 1 tsp butter
- 1 shallot, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, crushed
- 40g sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tbsp Italian herbs
- Pinch of chili flakes
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 100ml single light cream (like Elmlea)
- 200ml chicken stock
- Handful of fresh parsley, finely chopped
- 125g dry basmati rice per serving (to serve)
- Grated Parmesan (to garnish)

# Instructions

1. **Prep the Chicken:** Pat the chicken thighs dry, season generously with salt and pepper, and lightly coat them with the cornflour.
2. **Sear:** Heat the sun-dried tomato oil in a large, deep non-stick pan over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken thighs for 5-10 minutes until golden and crisp, then flip and cook for another 3-4 minutes. Remove the chicken and set aside.
3. **Sauté:** In the same pan, melt the butter and reduce the heat to medium. Sauté the shallot and garlic for 2-3 minutes, stirring regularly.
4. **Build the Sauce:** Add the chopped sun-dried tomatoes and cook for 1 minute. Stir in the smoked paprika, Italian herbs, chili flakes, and tomato paste. Cook for 2 minutes until the tomato paste thickens slightly.
5. **Simmer:** Pour in the cream and chicken stock, stir to combine, and bring to a low simmer. Season with a little salt and pepper, then stir in the fresh chopped parsley.
6. **Combine:** Return the seared chicken thighs to the pan, tossing to coat them in the creamy sauce. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is completely cooked through.
7. **Serve:** Serve the creamy Tuscan chicken over your cooked white basmati rice, and garnish with some extra fresh parsley and grated Parmesan!
