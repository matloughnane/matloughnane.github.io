This is a recipe found on instagram by [@ayline.ea](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DVUL5dajeHT/)
(Yields: 3-4 wraps)

# Ingredients

**For the Chipotle Chicken Marinade:**
- 600g chicken breasts (about 2 large), sliced into strips
- 3–4 chipotle peppers in adobo
- 4 garlic cloves
- 100g small onion, chopped
- 15g (1 tbsp) mayonnaise
- 30g (2 tbsp) Greek yogurt
- Juice of 1/2 lime
- 15g (1 tbsp) paprika
- 5g (1 tsp) chili powder
- 5g (1 tsp) garlic powder
- 5g (1 tsp) cumin
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

**For the Creamy Chipotle Sauce:**
- Half of the prepared chicken marinade (reserved *before* adding raw chicken)
- 30g (2 tbsp) mayonnaise

**For the Fresh Crunchy Salad:**
- 150g red onion, thinly sliced
- 200g medium cucumber, diced
- 150g red bell pepper, diced
- 15g fresh parsley, chopped
- 21g (1 tbsp) honey
- 15ml (1 tbsp) fresh lemon juice
- 15ml (1 tbsp) avocado oil (or olive oil)
- Salt, to taste

**For Assembly:**
- 3–4 large lavash breads or tortilla wraps (about 300g total)
- *Optional:* French fries, to put inside the wrap!

# Instructions

1. **Make the Marinade:** In a blender, combine the chipotle peppers, garlic, onion, mayo (15g), Greek yogurt, lime juice, paprika, chili powder, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Blend until completely smooth.
2. **Split the Marinade:** **Reserve exactly half** of the blended marinade in a separate bowl. This will become your sauce later.
3. **Marinate the Chicken:** Coat the sliced chicken strips with the *remaining* half of the marinade. (You can cook immediately, or refrigerate for up to 2 hours).
4. **Cook the Chicken:** Heat a skillet with a drizzle of oil over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and cook for 4–5 minutes per side, or until completely cooked through and slightly charred.
5. **Make the Creamy Sauce:** Take the clean, reserved half of the marinade and mix it with 30g of mayonnaise until smooth. Taste and adjust salt/lime juice as needed.
6. **Make the Salad:** In a medium bowl, combine the sliced red onion, diced cucumber, diced red bell pepper, and chopped parsley. Drizzle with the honey, lemon juice, and avocado oil. Season with salt and toss to combine.
7. **Assemble:** Lay a lavash flat. Spread a generous amount of the creamy chipotle sauce over it. Add the cooked chicken, a scoop of the crunchy salad, and some fries (if using). 
8. **Toast:** Roll it up tightly. Heat a dry skillet or panini press over medium heat and toast the wrap for 2–3 minutes per side until golden brown and crispy!
