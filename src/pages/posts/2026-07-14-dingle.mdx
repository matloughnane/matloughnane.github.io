We spent a few sunny days in Dingle, Kerry. We stayed in Ballyfitterer and the Slea Head drive was our main route. 

We travelled clockwise around the drive past the  Coumeenoole beach onto the Dunquin pier and day 2 featured the Gallarus Oratory.

An incredible place with a fantastic trip.
